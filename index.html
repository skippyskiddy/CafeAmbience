about

I'm Elif, a computer science student who has spent a lot of time working and experimenting with various methods to increase my productivity over the years. I created this project to combine some of my favorite elements from these experiments (backed by science, I promise). For an optimal experience, I recommend using Chrome.

Why combine all these things in one hub?

  • I'm a fan of to-do lists and wanted to integrate one right into my study environment instead of having to switch tabs and applications.
  • The Coffee Shop Effect: different scenes and soundscapes help me concentrate better, so I've included those options. I also wanted to replicate that cafe-productivity even when working from home.
  • Included is a Pomodoro timer and cycle tracker to remind you to take short breaks.

Feel free to download this project from GitHub and tailor the audio, video, or other elements to your preferences. All audio/video content is copyright-free.

Special thanks to imissmycafe.com and driveandlisten.com for the inspiration behind this project. If you're intrigued by their work, I think you'll enjoy what I've created here.
